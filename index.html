📥 Import Spreadsheet (CSV)

You can bulk-import specimens into your archive using a CSV (Comma-Separated Values) file. The importer supports flexible column header mapping:

Headers: Specimen Name | Type | Category | Period | Epoch | Stage | Formula | Crystal System | Hardness | Luster | Streak | Cleavage | Color | Size | Unit | Price | Wishlist | Sold | Dream
  • Required: Specimen Name (or Name) to identify the specimen.
  • Specimen Type: Type (set to fossil or mineral). Defaults to fossil.
  • Mineral Fields: Formula, Crystal System, Hardness (Mohs scale), Luster, Streak, Cleavage, Color.
  • Fossil Taxonomy & Origin: Category, Geological Period, Epoch, Stage, Country, Location, and Formation.
  • Status Flags: Mark items as Is Wishlist, Is Sold, or Is Dream with true or false.
  • Valuation & Size: Numeric values for Size, Weight, Price, and Sale Price.

Specimenry

A personal database for fossils and minerals — catalog, field notes, labels, and your collection story in one place.

  • Local-first & private. Specimens, photos, and field diary trips stay in your browser. No account required. Nothing is uploaded unless you choose optional sync.
  • Free to use. The app is free. Optional Ko-fi tips support development — there is no paid lock on core features.
  • Phone & desktop. In the field, use Simple mode when adding (name, photo, location, notes, trip). On desktop, use Advanced for full curation.

Backup & Restore

Download one JSON file you own. Restore it on this browser or another device.

What a backup includes

  • All specimens (fossils & minerals), photos, notes, tags, storage & prices
  • Field diary trips and specimen links
  • Provenance documents attached to specimens

Not included: optional Google Drive / P2P sync settings, and AI speech models (re-downloaded if needed).

— — — — —

Your data lives in this browser — export backups regularly.

Move to a new phone (3 steps)
  1. On this device: tap Download backup. Save the JSON file somewhere you can open on the new phone (Files, Drive, AirDrop, email to yourself).
  2. On the new phone: open specimenry.online in the browser you will keep using (Safari / Chrome). Prefer a clean profile so you do not merge with old leftovers.
  3. Restore: Database → Backup & Restore → Restore from file…, pick the JSON. Confirm the “Backup check: OK” summary, then wait for “Restore verified OK”.

Tip: after a successful restore on the new phone, download one fresh backup there too — that becomes the new “last backup OK”.

Quick smoke check (2 minutes)
  1. Add a test specimen in Simple mode (name + note), then delete or keep it.
  2. Download backup — confirm toast says Backup OK and the file appears in Downloads / Files.
  3. (Optional) Restore that file in a private/incognito window and confirm “Restore verified OK”.

Restore merges by specimen/trip ID (same ID overwrites). Prefer restoring into an empty browser profile when moving devices.
